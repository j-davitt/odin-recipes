Davitt's Special Bourbon

Description

Quite possibly the greatest drink for any occasion! Celebration? You got it! Mourning? Absolutely! Press conference? It's great there too!

Ingredients

  • 3 ice cubes
  • 1 bottle Woodford Reserve Bourbon

Steps

  1. In your favorite glass combine the ice cubes with a healthy pour of Woodford Reserve.
  2. Enjoy!
