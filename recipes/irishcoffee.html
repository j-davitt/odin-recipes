Irish Coffee

Description

Who said whiskey wasn't for breakfast?! This coffee delight is sure to get your morning going!

Ingredients

  • 1.5 fl oz Irish cream liquer
  • 1.5 fl oz Irish whiskey
  • 1 cup hot brewed coffee
  • 1 tablespoon whipped cream
  • 1 dash ground nutmeg

Steps

  1. In a coffee mug, combine Irish cream and whiskey. Fill mug with coffee. Top with dab of whipped cream and a dash of nutmeg.
