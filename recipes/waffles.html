Waffles

Description

The classic crispy waffle everyone loves!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1.5 cups warm milk
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix together flour, salt, baking powder, and sugar; set aside. Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature.
  2. In a seperate bowl, beat the eggs. Stir in the milk, butter, and vanilla. Pour the milk mixture into the flour mixture; beat until blended.
  3. Ladle the batter into a preheated waffle iron. Cook the waffles until gold and crisp. Serve immediately.
